Our Mission

What N.O.I.S.E. 385 Stands For

We believe every member deserves leadership that is present, accountable, and committed to the workers — not just during election season, but every single day.

🔊
Transparency
Members deserve to know what's happening — decisions, finances, negotiations. No more information gatekeeping.
⚖️
Equal Representation
Every member gets the same level of support. No favorites, no shortcuts, no exceptions. Fair across the board.
✊
Accountability
Leadership should be measured by action, not promises. We hold leaders accountable to the members they serve.
