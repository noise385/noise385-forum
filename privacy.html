Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

N.O.I.S.E. 385 respects your privacy and will never sell or share your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes.

This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when joining our movement or communicating with us.

Email Communications

By providing your email address, you agree to receive updates related to union matters, campaigns, and N.O.I.S.E. 385 activities.

You may unsubscribe at any time using the unsubscribe link provided in any of our emails. We will honor all opt-out requests promptly.

SMS Text Messages

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive SMS messages related to union updates and N.O.I.S.E. 385 activities.

Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for assistance.

Consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of participation in N.O.I.S.E. 385.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you join or interact with N.O.I.S.E. 385:

• Full name
• Email address
• Phone number
• City/Location
• Messages or communications you send us

This information is used solely to communicate with you about union matters and N.O.I.S.E. 385 activities.

How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Your information is stored securely and access is limited to authorized N.O.I.S.E. 385 personnel only.
